System Architecture

The "Stupid Simple" Philosophy

SEND IT IN was engineered to solve one specific problem: field workers breaking Google Sheets. We decouple data entry from data storage.

  • Progressive Web App (PWA) architecture.
  • No App Store downloads required.
  • Instant sync via Google Apps Script.

Data Security

Where does my data go?
Directly into a private Google Sheet owned by your business. We do not host your proprietary data on third-party servers.

Are the worker PINs secure?
Yes. PINs are verified against a master routing log to ensure data drops into the correct client ledger.
