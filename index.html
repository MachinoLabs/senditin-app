Field Guide

  • Assignment
    Job Site, Unit Name, or Project Title.
  • Quantity / Value
    Track Hours, Parts, Gallons, and/or Dollar amounts.
  • Reference #
    Link Entry to a specific PO, Ticket ID, or Invoice Number.
  • Notes
    Provide specific descriptions, list issues, and/or dictate a summary of action performed.
  • Task Complete?
    Toggle YES to close the job - NO if follow-up is needed.

Tips for Use

  • 🎤 Use Your Voice
    Tap the microphone on your keyboard to dictate entries.
  • 🏠 Add to Home Screen
    Add it to your home screen for one-tap access.
  • ☀️ Switch for Sunlight
    Use the ☀️/🌙 toggle to switch modes. Light mode is easier to see in bright conditions.
  • 📶 Send It Anyway
    No signal? Hit SEND IT IN. The app holds your entry and sends it automatically once you're back online.
