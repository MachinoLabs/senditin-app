Direct Manager Control

This dashboard pulls data directly from your specific client spreadsheet, allowing you to review field reports in real-time.

  • Instantly updates on refresh.
  • Direct map linking to GPS coordinates.
  • Clean, distraction-free interface.

Field Data Guide

  • Assignment: The site or project name.
  • Location: GPS link captured at the moment of submission.
  • Agent: The internal ID or name of the worker who submitted the report.

Dashboard Tips

Is this a live feed?
Yes, the moment a field worker gets a cellular signal and their report clears the background sync, it will appear here upon refresh.

Hard to read in a bright truck?
Use the ☀️ icon in the top right header to switch to Light Mode for high-glare environments.
